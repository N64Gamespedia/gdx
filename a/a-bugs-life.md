_**A Bug's Life**_ is a third-person action platformer based on the Disney/Pixar animated film. Players control Flik, an inventive ant, as he journeys through environments inspired by the movie to save his colony from the villainous grasshoppers. Gameplay includes collecting grain, spelling out F-L-I-K for bonuses, transforming seeds into helpful plants, and using berries as projectile weapons.
